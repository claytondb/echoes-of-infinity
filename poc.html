Echoes of Infinity — Changelog

v0.6.0
Gauntlet system with 5 Power Stone slots — equip stones to unlock abilities
Invisibility Cloak stone (Echo 1): press Q to become invisible for 5 seconds
Time Warp stone (Echo 2): turn-based battle system with strategic actions
Tree leaf canopies with LEAF tile blocks (non-solid, breakable)
Portal transition animations: cinematic zoom out, multiverse view, zoom in
Defeated echo indicators on multiverse branches
v0.5.0
Consistent spawn position across multiverses — player keeps XY coordinates when traveling, blocks cleared if needed
Camera zooms in smoothly when approaching an Echo for combat
Click to attack (replaces SHIFT) — hold click for continuous attacks
Smoother block step-up: character moves up then forward instead of clipping through
v0.4.0
Step-up animation speed matches walking pace
Multiverse viewer only shows previously visited branches
Hold-to-mine: continuous mining while mouse is held down
Smooth tweening when stepping up blocks
v0.3.0
Plants render as 🌿 emoji pickups instead of green blocks, reduced spawn rate
Auto-step up single-block ledges without jumping
Walk animation with swinging arms and legs
Character faces movement direction
v0.2.0
Multiverse slice viewer — edge-on 3D sheets of glass showing real world snapshots
Click-to-travel in multiverse viewer
Player ghost outline overlay on non-origin branches
Thinner, more angled slices with subtle perspective
v0.1.0
